Sound Familiar
The conversation dies.
You never know why.

It's not your looks. It's not your status. It's timing, tone, and tension — and most guys get all three wrong without realizing it.

"I replied too fast and she went cold."

You had high investment — she was engaged. Replying instantly collapsed the tension. Reactance dropped. She lost interest before you said anything wrong.

"I tried playing it cool and lost momentum."

You waited too long. She read the silence as disinterest, not mystery. The window closed. There's an optimal delay — and it's not "as long as possible."

"I can't tell if she's losing interest or just busy."

One-word replies mean different things at different moments. Investment scoring tells you which one this is before you respond and make it worse.

"I said the right thing at the wrong time."

The message wasn't the problem — the sequence was. Attraction is readable and manageable if you have the right framework. You just never had it until now.
