SECTION 01

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account, use the Service, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address, username, and password (or OAuth token for Sign in with Apple / Google).
  • Onboarding responses: answers to setup questions used to configure your coaching profile (e.g., communication style preferences, goals). These responses are stored in your user profile and used to personalize coaching outputs.
  • Coaching session content: text messages you submit to the AI coach, conversation context you type or paste into the app, and coaching responses generated on your behalf.
  • Profile optimizer inputs: dating profile text, bio content, and written prompts you submit for analysis via the Profile & Bio Optimizer feature.
  • Support communications: any messages or attachments you send to our support team.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we automatically collect certain technical and behavioral data:

  • Device and app data: device type, operating system version, app version, device identifiers (not advertising IDs), time zone, and locale.
  • Usage analytics: screens viewed, features used, session duration, button interactions, and coaching session counts. We use Mixpanel for analytics. Event data is pseudonymized and does not include the content of your coaching sessions.
  • Error and performance data: crash reports, stack traces, and performance metrics collected via Sentry. These logs do not include conversation content but may include session identifiers.
  • Subscription status: subscription tier, trial status, and entitlement data managed via RevenueCat. We do not collect or store payment card information.
  • Push notification tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens used to deliver push notifications, including Daily Rizz Challenges and coaching reminders.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

Screenshot Data — Critical Disclosure Photographs and screenshots you upload for conversation analysis via the Vision Analysis feature are processed in memory by the OpenAI GPT-4o Vision API. These images are never written to our database, never stored on our servers, and never retained by 413 Vision LLC after processing is complete. Images are transmitted directly to the OpenAI API over TLS and are subject to OpenAI's data processing policies. See Section 3 for the full disclosure.

We do not collect:

  • Your actual dating app messages, the identity of any third party whose messages you submit, or any personal information about third parties.
  • Payment card numbers, bank account information, or full billing details (handled exclusively by Apple App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat).
  • Precise GPS location data.
  • Contacts, calendar, or microphone data.
  • Social media account credentials.

2.1 Persistent Memory via Vector Embeddings

To improve coaching continuity across sessions, Rizz IQ maintains a persistent memory layer powered by pgvector (a PostgreSQL extension). When you use the coaching features, selected context from prior sessions is converted into numerical embeddings and stored in our database. These embeddings allow the AI coach to recall relevant context from prior conversations.

Embeddings are stored in association with your user account and are deleted when you delete your account. You may request deletion of your coaching memory at any time via Settings → Privacy → Clear Coaching Memory, without deleting your full account.

2.2 We Do Not Train AI Models on Your Data

Your coaching session content, onboarding responses, and submitted text are never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI model operated by 413 Vision LLC or any third-party AI provider, except as limited by the applicable sub-processor policies disclosed in Section 4. We do not use your personal data as training data for large language models.

SECTION 03

Screenshot & Image Processing

Important — Third Party in Image Processing Flow When you upload screenshots using the Vision Analysis feature, those images are transmitted to OpenAI's API for processing. 413 Vision LLC does not store the images, but they do pass through OpenAI's systems. By using the screenshot upload feature, you acknowledge this transmission.

3.1 How Screenshot Analysis Works

The Vision Analysis feature allows you to upload screenshots of dating app conversations. The following describes the complete technical flow:

  1. You select up to 5 images from your device's local storage using the native OS image picker.
  2. Images are uploaded over an encrypted (TLS 1.2+) connection to a Supabase Edge Function operated by 413 Vision LLC.
  3. The Edge Function immediately forwards the image data to the OpenAI GPT-4o Vision API for text extraction and conversation analysis.
  4. OpenAI returns a structured text response (extracted conversation content, speaker attribution, platform detection).
  5. Images are not written to any database or persistent storage. They exist only in server memory during the duration of the API call, which typically completes in under 10 seconds.
  6. The structured text output (not the image) is then processed by our coaching logic and stored as part of your coaching session record.

3.2 What You Should Know Before Uploading Screenshots

  • Screenshots may contain the name, username, profile photo, and written messages of a third party (the person you are talking to). You are solely responsible for ensuring that uploading such content complies with the terms of service of the originating platform and applicable law.
  • Do not upload screenshots containing sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for conversation coaching (e.g., do not upload screenshots containing financial information, Social Security numbers, or health data).
  • OpenAI's API data usage policies apply to image content during transmission. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data in API requests.

3.3 Profile Optimizer Text Content

The Profile & Bio Optimizer feature accepts text input only (profile bios, prompts, headlines). This text is processed by the Anthropic Claude API. No images are used in the Profile Optimizer. Text input is not stored beyond the active session unless you save an analysis result to your profile audit history.

SECTION 07

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit.
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest in Supabase (PostgreSQL).
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data.
  • JWT-based authentication with token expiry and refresh rotation.
  • Access controls limiting internal access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security dependency audits via automated tooling.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we implement commercially reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

SECTION 10

Push Notifications

Rizz IQ may send push notifications to your device, including Daily Rizz Challenges, coaching reminders, streak notifications, and product updates. Push notifications require your explicit permission, which is requested at first launch via a native OS permission dialog.

You may revoke push notification permissions at any time through your device operating system settings (iOS: Settings → Notifications → Rizz IQ; Android: Settings → Apps → Rizz IQ → Notifications). Revoking permissions does not affect your ability to use any other feature of the Service.

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and Expo Notifications to deliver push notifications. Your FCM device token is stored in our database associated with your account and is deleted when you delete your account or revoke notification permissions.
